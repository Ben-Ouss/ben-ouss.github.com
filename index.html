Hello !

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at Université du Québec à Montréal (UQAM) under the supervision of professor Frédéric Rochon. I obtained my Bachelor degree from Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 (UCBL) and my Masters degree from Sorbonne Université. You can find my CV here .

The goals of my PhD are twofold: first, to study various analytic aspects of conical degenerations of Calabi–Yau manifolds; and second, to develop a uniform construction of the resolvent of the Hodge Laplacian for conical degenerations of Riemannian manifolds.
